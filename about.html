Falooda and Shakes is all about Best Quality, Right Quantity at Affordable Pricing. One stop solution for all your food cravings. It's kind of mini Food court, where one can get Shakes, Falooda, Rabri, Ice-cream. All in different flavours Smoothies, Kulfies, Milkshakes/ Thickshakes and also traditional drinks like Kesar-pista

One can run it as Commercial Quick Service Cafe or as a proper dine in Cafe. We secure the quality with our secret and simplified recipes, which helps in making drinks quickly and with same taste repeatedly.

Objective with Falooda zone is to offer best quality food at affordable prices. A family of 4 people can fulfil their Cravings under Rs.500 at any Faloodazone store. Our Menu offers 4 categories with around 28 items of food and beverages. Average ticket value is ₹80 and gross profit margin of 60-70%. our secret is pocket friendly pricing gives secured business in all seasons.

Launched in May 2022, Falooda zone franchise call for an investment of ₹3.5 Lakhs for ExpressModel which offers beverages Along with falooda,Rabri and Ice-cream. In this amount The brand offers free training, Billing Software, Free Marketing, total Equipments are included along with Branding and Design of store.
